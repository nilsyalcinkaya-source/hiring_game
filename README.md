Hi there!   I’m very curious about the reasons of quick rejections in today’s competitive job market. 

Is it because of the age? Too much career gap? Not so "fancy" company names in the CV? Or, do I miss something else? Or, on the positive side, who is the one who is preferred the most? Which profiles were the “ultimate winners”?

So, instead of making a boring survey which will end up results full of biases, I made this little game based on a "Bracketed Choice" or in other words, a "Single Elimination Tournament" format test. 

At the end, I didn't focus solely on my strenghts or weaknesses; instead, I wanted to find out some more general hints about the hiring world's point of view. So I added some extra skills or weaknesses to represent potential job seekers as well. 

a) If you are reading this message as an HR professional, have fun and don't forget to share it with your colleagues. 
b) If you are not an HR professional but familiar with hiring expectations within your industry (especially tech, UX design etc.) you can play it too. 
c) But if you are not one of the above, I kindly ask you to just have a look but please don't finish the 10 rounds, so that I can have a clearer research data at the end. 

(Please keep in mind that I'm not a software developer and I used Gemini to design the game. But at least I wanted to show how I miss 90s with this retro style choice :) ) 

Thank you very much for your participation!
