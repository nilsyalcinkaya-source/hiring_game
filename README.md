I was curious about the reasons why I keep being rejected. 
Is it because I'm too old? Too much career gap as a mom of two? Not so "fancy" company names in my CV? Or, do I miss something else?

So, instead of making a boring survey, I made this little game based on a "Bracketed Choice" or in other words, 
a "Single Elimination Tournament" format test. 

At the end, I didn't focus solely on my strenghts or weaknesses; instead, I wanted to find out some more general hints about the hiring
world's point of view. So I added some extra skills or weaknesses to represent potential job seekers as well. 

a) If you are reading this message as an HR professional, have fun and don't forget to share it with your colleagues. 
b) If you are not an HR professional but familiar with hiring expectations within your industry (especially tech, UX design etc.) you can play it too. 
c) But if you are not one of the above, I kindly ask you to just have a look but please don't finish the 10 rounds, 
so that I can have a clearer research data at the end. 

(Please keep in mind that I'm not a software developer and I used Gemini to design the game. But at least I wanted to show how I miss 90s with this retro style choice :) ) 

Thank you very much for your participation!
